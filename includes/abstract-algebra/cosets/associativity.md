Let $H$ be a subset of $G$.
Let $a, b \in G$. Then $a(bH) = (ab)H$ and $(aH)b = a(Hb)$.

## Proof

\begin{align}
& a(bH)
\\ &= a\{bh: h \in H\}
\\ &= \{a(bh): h \in H\}
\\ &= \{(ab)h: h \in H\}
\\ &= (ab)H
\end{align}

\begin{align}
& (aH)b
\\ &= \{ah: h \in H\}b
\\ &= \{(ah)b: h \in H\}
\\ &= \{a(hb): h \in H\}
\\ &= a\{hb: h \in H\}
\\ &= a(Hb)
\end{align}
