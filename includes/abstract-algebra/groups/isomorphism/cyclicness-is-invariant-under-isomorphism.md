If $\langle a \rangle \cong H$ via $\phi$, then $H = \langle \phi(a) \rangle$.
This means that $H$ is cyclic.

## Proof

\begin{align}
& g \in \langle \phi(a) \rangle
\\ &\iff \exists k, g = \phi(a)^k
\\ &\iff \exists k, g = \phi(a^k)
\\ &\iff g \in \phi(\langle a \rangle) = H
\end{align}

Therefore, $\langle \phi(a) \rangle = H$.
